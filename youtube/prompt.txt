Graduation
Graduation is a term describing the end of activity of a VTuber. The term was inherited from the Japanese Idol industry where after a certain amount of time or due to circumstance, an idol would "graduate" from their group which result in the end of their activities as a member of that group. In the context of VTuber, a graduation entail a total retirement of the specific VTuber identity including an abandonment or rebranding of their channel(s). In the context Hololive Production, a graduation is one of the three ways a talent can stop their activity as a member and relinquish their HoloPro identity back to Cover Corp. Specifically, Graduation is mutual end of agreement between Cover and their Talents with the implication that the fixed-term contract between the two have ended without renewal.
The other two ways in which a HoloPro member can end their activity is by Termination (in which Cover unilaterally end the contract after the talent violate a rule or term) and Ending-of-Contract (in which the talent choose to end their contract before the end of term due to personal reasons)
Note that while graduation can be considered the death of a VTuber character, the person behind the avatar are free to continue their life including returning as a completely new identity, as VTuber or otherwise.

VShojo
Formed in 2020, VShojo was a VTuber group based in the United States and one of the first VTuber groups based outside Japan. The EN Hololive members occasionally collaborated with members of VShojo. They were widely considered one of the major vtuber agency especially in the English speaking world and especially on Twitch. In July 2025 Ironmouse left VShojo, revealing that the company was withholding half a million dollars in charity money and "significant amounts" of money was being withheld from Ironmouse herself. The controversy led to the remaining members resigning and revealing their own poor experiences with VShojo. Justin 'theGunrun' Ignacio, the CEO of VShojo announced the company shutting down on July 25th.
